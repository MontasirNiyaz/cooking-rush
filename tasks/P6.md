# P6 — Social economy & monetization
Designs: docs/COOKING_GAME_ROADMAP_M7-M12.md §M9, M11, M12.

## P6.1 Idle empire
**Steps:** `IdleMath.accrue(state, elapsed)` pure+tested; `IdleService` computes accrual
from profile timestamps on join (server clamps elapsed; never client clock); offline cap
upgradeable (config); chefs assignable to buildings; spatial collect interaction at hub
plots with the P1.4 coin-burst juice.
**Accept:** accrual unit tests incl. cap + clamp + assignment bonuses; collect is
idempotent (double-fire safe); timestamps survive rejoin.

## P6.2 Trading
**Steps:** `TradeService`: both-confirm two-phase flow with final review lock; server
escrow; eligibility gates (account age, playtime, level — constants in config);
`tradeable` flag respected from item configs; full trade log (DataStore append);
rate limits; every grant idempotent.
**Accept:** adversarial tests — confirm-spam, mid-trade disconnect, item-swap-after-
confirm, duplicate-grant replay — all safe; untradeable items cannot enter the window.

## P6.3 Full monetization
**Steps:** Expand Cosmetics.lua (decor themes per restaurant, serve VFX skins, chef
skins); convenience gamepasses (offline cap+, equip slot+, auto-collect, 2x mastery XP);
gem dev-products; all odds/prices published in UI.
**Accept:** nothing purchasable closes a skill gap or is unearnable free (audit table
committed to docs/); ProcessReceipt paths idempotent; refund-safe grant records.
